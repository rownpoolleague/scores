R-OWN Pool League Rules

League Administration

1. League Officers

  • League Operator
  • Team Captains
  • Participating Sponsors

Each League Officer has one vote in all League matters.

2. League Divisions

  • Maximum of 16 teams per division
  • 16-week sessions including Position Week
  • New teams/divisions require League Officer approval

3. Fees

  • $60 Sponsor fee due week one
  • $15 per player per week (16 weeks = $300)

4. Trophies & Prize Money

  • Trophies awarded to 1st place teams
  • Prize money paid to every team

5. Sponsors

Sponsors must maintain tables within a 2" roll variance.

Player Conduct

1. Conduct of Players

  • Physical Contact: Fight starters forfeit all 5 team points and may be expelled
  • Verbal Abuse: Match forfeiture; continued abuse may forfeit all points
  • Unsportsmanlike Conduct: Warning → match forfeiture
  • Cheating: Individual match forfeiture; captains lose all points
  • Age: Legal drinking age required

Forfeits & Scheduling

  • Match start time: 7:00 PM SHARP
  • 1 point forfeited every 5 minutes late until 7:25 PM
  • Intentional forfeits in last 2 weeks may lose all prize money
  • BYE weeks award 3 points
  • Captains may mutually agree to break any rule

Disputes & Protests

  • Only captains may intervene
  • Continue play under protest
  • League Operator decision is final

Match Play Rules

  • Coin-operated tables: costs split
  • Player selection determined by coin flip
  • One designated coach per game
  • Coaching limited to 2 minutes (3 = foul)

Team Rosters

  • Minimum 5 players, maximum 8
  • New players added within first 6 weeks
  • Players may shoot twice only once per session
  • No shooting twice during position round

Position Rounds

Teams play based on standings (1v2, 3v4, etc). Head-to-head is first tiebreaker.

General Game Rules

  • Game: 8-Ball
  • 8-Ball must be called and pocketed separately
  • Legal break requires 4 balls to rails or a pocketed ball
  • Jump shots over 45° are fouls
  • Ball-in-hand on all fouls

Handicap System

Handicaps are based on best 5 of last 10 matches.

  • Less than 2 innings = 7
  • Less than 3 = 6
  • Less than 4 = 5
  • Less than 6 = 4
  • Less than 8 = 3
  • 8 or more = 2

Maximum Team Handicap

  • Max combined handicap: 23
  • Violation = forfeiture of all 5 points

Paperwork & Scoring

  • Innings, safeties, scratches must be marked
  • Score sheets returned by 7:00 PM next day
  • Clear photo acceptable if not returned physically
Cell phones and earbuds may NOT be used during your shot.
Any activity causing delay over 1 minute must be agreed upon in advance.
HAVE FUN!!
